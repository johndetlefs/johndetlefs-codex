# Ecommerce Buying Cycle Script

## Video Promise

By the end of this video, a Shopify store owner should understand the basic buying cycle and be able to look at their store as a sales journey, not just a collection of pages and products.

## Opening Hook

Preferred hook direction:

Would you believe most people on the Central Coast actually do want to support local brands? But there is a limit to how Dodgy Brothers they are willing to go.

If your Shopify store feels unclear, risky, hard to navigate, or like nobody is quite sure what happens after they enter their credit card details, that local goodwill disappears pretty quickly.

So today we are going to look at the ecommerce buying cycle: the steps your store needs to take a customer through before they are comfortable buying from you.

See `hooks.md` for alternate opening tests.

## Structure

1. The physical-store comparison.
2. The wrong message at the wrong time can kill a sale.
3. What the buying cycle means in plain language.
4. How each stage maps to a Shopify store.
5. Where small stores usually lose people.
6. How to review your own store.
7. Fix-It Call CTA.

## Full Script

To be drafted after the brief/framework is approved.

Early note to include:

If you market the wrong message at the wrong time, you can kill a sale. This is where Klaviyo, Shopify Email, Mailchimp, SMS, abandoned cart emails, browse abandonment, and post-purchase flows need to match behaviour.

An abandoned cart discount might be useful in some situations, but it is not automatically the right answer. If the customer left because they did not trust the delivery timing, did not understand sizing, were unsure about compatibility, or had not seen enough proof, a discount does not fix the real problem. The message has to match where they are in the buying cycle.
