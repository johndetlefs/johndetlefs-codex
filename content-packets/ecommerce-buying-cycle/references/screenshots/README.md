# Ecommerce Buying Cycle Screenshots

Drop proof screenshots for this guide into the brand folders below.

## Folders

- `the-good-guys/`
- `koala/`
- `good-pair-days/`

## Useful Captures

### The Good Guys

- Search/product discovery.
- Product page price, trust, delivery, pickup, and finance/payment reassurance.
- Price-match, cheaper-price, or automated price-checking messaging.
- Related/similar product suggestions.

### Koala

- Product page near add-to-cart.
- Reviews and social proof.
- Trial, warranty, delivery, returns, comfort, and quality reassurance.
- Sticky buy section if visible.

### Good Pair Days

- Mobile product page where possible.
- Wine education: acidity, body, tasting notes, profile, or similar indicators.
- Recipes/pairings.
- Reviews.
- Repeat purchase, rewards, subscriptions, app, or rating mechanisms.
- Any section that makes wine feel approachable rather than snooty.

## Naming

Use short descriptive names, for example:

- `price-trust-delivery.png`
- `search-related-products.png`
- `reviews-trial-warranty.png`
- `wine-info-mobile.png`
- `recipes-reviews.png`
- `repeat-buying-rewards.png`
