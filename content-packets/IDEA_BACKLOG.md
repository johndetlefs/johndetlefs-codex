# Guide Video Idea Backlog

Use this file for video ideas that spin off while planning, scripting, recording, editing, or turning a guide into a page. Keep entries brief until they are ready to become their own `content-packets/<topic-slug>/` packet.

## How To Use

- Add ideas as soon as they appear, even if they are rough.
- Keep the description short: audience, promise, and why it matters.
- Link back to the source packet when the idea came from another guide.
- Promote an idea into its own packet when it is ready for briefing.

## Backlog

### Matching Shopify Emails To The Buying Cycle

- Status: idea
- Source packet: `content-packets/ecommerce-buying-cycle`
- Working angle: Klaviyo, Shopify Email, Mailchimp, SMS, abandoned cart, browse abandonment, and post-purchase flows only work properly when the message matches where the customer is in the buying cycle.
- Why it matters: many stores send discounts when customers actually need answers, proof, delivery clarity, product education, sizing help, or replenishment reminders.
- Possible hooks:
  - "Stop sending discounts when customers need answers."
  - "The wrong email at the wrong time can kill a sale."
  - "An abandoned cart discount is not always the answer."
- Possible proof/examples: abandoned cart, browse abandonment, post-purchase, replenishment, wine/premium food club or reorder flows, sizing/fit education, delivery reassurance.
