# Quick First Read

## Metadata

- Lead magnet: Shopify Bottleneck Analysis
- Angle: fast useful result
- Target length: 25-35 seconds
- Placement: Meta/Instagram
- Destination: `/shopify-bottleneck-analysis`
- CTA: "Run the free Shopify Bottleneck Analysis."

## Hook

"You do not need a full agency audit to stop guessing where your Shopify funnel is weak."

## Purpose

Make the analysis feel low-friction, useful, and concrete enough to earn the click.

## Spoken Script

You do not need a full agency audit to stop guessing where your Shopify funnel is weak.

You need a useful first read.

The Shopify Bottleneck Analysis looks at the core steps in your funnel: sessions, add to carts, reached checkout, and completed purchases.

Then it points to the likely weak spot, so you know whether to start with the product page, the cart, checkout, or a different growth lever.

It is free, and it gives you a clearer starting point before you start changing things at random.

Run the free Shopify Bottleneck Analysis and find where to look first.

## Remotion Overlay Plan

- Presenter/A-roll role: John should feel calm and credible, offering a simpler first step before bigger work.
- Lower thirds: "A useful first read" and "Find where to look first".
- Key phrase captions: "stop guessing", "core funnel steps", "clearer starting point".
- Visual cutaways: four metric cards animate in; a simple result card reveals "likely weak spot"; stage cards slide in for product page, cart, checkout, growth lever.
- CTA card: "Run the free Shopify Bottleneck Analysis."
- Continuity notes: use the same metric-card, stage-card, lower-third, and CTA-card treatment planned for the landing page and result videos.

## Shot Notes

- Keep pace steady and reassuring.
- This is the least aggressive ad angle. It should feel like a helpful invitation.

## Captions

Burned-in captions recommended for Meta placements.

## Claims To Avoid

- Do not imply the report replaces a real diagnostic.
- Do not promise a specific completion time unless the current app flow reliably supports that claim.
- Do not sell the paid diagnostic in this ad.
