# More Traffic Trap

## Metadata

- Lead magnet: Shopify Bottleneck Analysis
- Angle: wasted effort
- Target length: 25-35 seconds
- Placement: Meta/Instagram
- Destination: `/shopify-bottleneck-analysis`
- CTA: "Run the free Shopify Bottleneck Analysis."

## Hook

"If your store gets traffic but not enough orders, more traffic may just send more people into the same leak."

## Purpose

Reframe the merchant's growth problem from "get more visitors" to "find the stage that is wasting the visitors you already have."

## Spoken Script

If your Shopify store gets traffic but not enough orders, more traffic may not be the first fix.

It might just send more people into the same leak.

The question is: where are people dropping out?

Are they not adding to cart?

Are they adding to cart but not reaching checkout?

Or are they reaching checkout and still not buying?

The free Shopify Bottleneck Analysis helps you find the likely weak stage, so you are not guessing which part of the store to fix first.

Run it now and find the leak before you spend more getting people in.

## Remotion Overlay Plan

- Presenter/A-roll role: John speaks directly to the frustration of traffic not converting.
- Lower thirds: "More traffic is not always the first fix" and "Find the weak stage first".
- Key phrase captions: "same leak", "where are people dropping out?", "before you spend more".
- Visual cutaways: visitor dots entering a funnel and dropping out at different stages; ad spend arrow feeding the top of the funnel; leak marker appears before the CTA.
- CTA card: "Run the free Shopify Bottleneck Analysis."
- Continuity notes: use the same funnel rail, visitor-dot, lower-third, and CTA-card treatment planned for the landing page and result videos.

## Shot Notes

- Tone should be practical, not anti-ads.
- Do not sound like paid traffic is bad. The point is sequencing: diagnose before scaling.

## Captions

Burned-in captions recommended for Meta placements.

## Claims To Avoid

- Do not say more traffic is always wrong.
- Do not claim the report proves ad quality is the issue.
- Do not guarantee revenue recovery.
