# Checkout Myth

## Metadata

- Lead magnet: Shopify Bottleneck Analysis
- Angle: myth reversal
- Target length: 30-40 seconds
- Placement: Meta/Instagram
- Destination: `/shopify-bottleneck-analysis`
- CTA: "Run the free Shopify Bottleneck Analysis."

## Hook

"Most Shopify owners think checkout is the problem. But for a lot of stores, the leak starts before checkout ever gets a chance."

## Purpose

Create curiosity around the actual funnel stage before the merchant starts changing checkout, payment, or abandoned cart tactics.

## Spoken Script

Most Shopify owners think checkout is the problem.

And sometimes it is.

But for a lot of stores, the bigger leak starts before checkout ever gets a chance.

People visit the store, look at the product, and never become interested enough to add to cart.

Or they add to cart, then stall before checkout.

So if you start by fixing checkout, you might be working on the wrong part of the funnel.

I built a free Shopify Bottleneck Analysis that helps you find the likely leak: product page, cart, checkout, or no obvious major leak.

Run the free analysis, and find the stage to look at first.

## Remotion Overlay Plan

- Presenter/A-roll role: John delivers the hook and explains the mistaken assumption directly to camera.
- Lower thirds: "Free Shopify Bottleneck Analysis" and "Where is the leak actually starting?"
- Key phrase captions: "checkout is the problem", "before checkout ever gets a chance", "find the stage first".
- Visual cutaways: funnel rail showing sessions, add to carts, reached checkout, completed purchases; checkout stage gets a question mark, then earlier stages highlight.
- CTA card: "Run the free Shopify Bottleneck Analysis" with `/shopify-bottleneck-analysis`.
- Continuity notes: use the same funnel rail, stage cards, lower-third style, and CTA-card treatment planned for the landing page and result videos.

## Shot Notes

- Start with direct eye contact.
- Keep the first line tight and slightly challenging.
- Leave a pause after "And sometimes it is" before the reversal.

## Captions

Burned-in captions recommended for Meta placements.

## Claims To Avoid

- Do not say checkout is never the problem.
- Do not imply the free report finds the exact cause.
- Do not sell the paid diagnostic in this ad.
