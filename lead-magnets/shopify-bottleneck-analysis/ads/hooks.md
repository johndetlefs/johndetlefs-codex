# Shopify Bottleneck Analysis Ad Hooks

## Hook Strategy

These hooks sell the free analysis before signup. They should create enough tension for the viewer to run the report.

Default structure is three Meta acquisition videos:

1. checkout myth / belief reversal;
2. more traffic trap / wasted effort;
3. quick first read / fast useful result.

The curiosity gap is:

```text
Which part of my funnel is actually leaking?
```

## Hook Options By Angle

### Checkout Myth

1. "Most Shopify owners think checkout is the problem. But for a lot of stores, the leak starts before checkout ever gets a chance."
2. "Before you fix checkout, check whether shoppers are even getting that far."
3. "If your store is not converting, checkout is the obvious suspect. It is not always the right one."

### More Traffic Trap

1. "If your store gets traffic but not enough orders, more traffic may just send more people into the same leak."
2. "More visitors will not fix a funnel leak if you do not know where the leak starts."
3. "Before you spend more on ads, find out which part of the store is losing the sale."

### Quick First Read

1. "In a few minutes, you can get a clearer answer than another week of guessing at your Shopify store."
2. "If you have been changing your store at random, start with this: find the stage that is actually leaking."
3. "You do not need a full agency audit to stop guessing where the funnel is weak."
