## Decisions

- Ad videos sell the free Shopify Bottleneck Analysis before signup.
- Result-page diagnostic videos remain in `../videos/` and sell the paid diagnostic after the user sees their result.
- Format is presenter-led talking head with Remotion motion-graphic overlays.
- Screen capture is not required for cold ad scripts unless a later demo variant needs it.
- Primary CTA: "Run the free Shopify Bottleneck Analysis."
- Default three-ad set: checkout myth / belief reversal, more traffic trap / wasted effort, and quick first read / fast useful result.
- Primary ad scripts should aim for 45-75 seconds and stay under 120 seconds.
- Ads must line up with the same journey promise used by the landing page and result videos: find the likely weak funnel stage before choosing what to fix.
