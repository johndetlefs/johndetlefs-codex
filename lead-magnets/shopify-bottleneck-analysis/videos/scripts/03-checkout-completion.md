## Purpose

Explain why shoppers may reach checkout but fail to complete the purchase, and frame checkout as a revenue recovery problem.

## Core Message

"These people were close to buying. Small checkout fixes can have a very direct revenue impact."

## Selected Hook

"Most funnel leaks are frustrating, but this one is especially expensive. You already earned the visit, the product interest, the cart action, and the checkout start. So why does the funnel lose people when buying intent should be highest?"

## Diagnostic Arc

- Immediate reveal: this is an expensive leak because the shopper is already close to buying.
- Diagnosis: something in checkout, shipping, payment, trust, or timing is stopping completion.
- Cost of inaction: the store has already paid for the visit and earned high intent, then loses the order at the final decision point.
- Fix path: inspect shipping, delivery promises, express payments, mobile checkout, abandoned checkout recovery, and any checkout customisation.
- Paid next step: use the diagnostic to inspect the checkout path before making random changes.
- Close: "Book my $295 Shopify Fix-It Diagnostic."

## Likely Causes

- Shipping rates are too high or surprising.
- Delivery timeframes are unclear.
- Payment methods are missing.
- No Shop Pay, PayPal, Apple Pay, or Google Pay where relevant.
- Address or payment friction.
- Weak abandoned checkout recovery.

## First Checks

- Shipping rates and delivery promises.
- Payment options.
- Checkout on mobile.
- Abandoned checkout emails or SMS.
- Checkout customisations or apps.

## Next Action

Treat checkout as a revenue recovery problem, not just a design problem.
