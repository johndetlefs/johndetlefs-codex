# <Lead Magnet Name> Ad Hooks

## Hook Strategy

Ad hooks should sell attention before they sell the report.

Use this pattern:

1. Belief: name what the viewer probably thinks.
2. Reversal: turn it over.
3. Gap: make them want the lead magnet.
4. CTA bridge: point to the free analysis.
