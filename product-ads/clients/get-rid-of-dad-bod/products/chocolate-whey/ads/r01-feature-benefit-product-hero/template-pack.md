# Get Rid Of Dad Bod R01 Template Pack

Product: Chocolate Whey Protein Concentrate

## Creative Job

The ad uses the R01 source pattern to make the product feel like an easy, repeatable support habit. The central product pack is the answer; the callouts reduce friction by showing protein, low sugar, grass-fed source, and simple prep around the pack.
