## What The Ad Is Doing

This format turns an ad into a private conversation. The viewer reads it like social proof because it resembles a reply from someone who noticed a change or asked a direct question.

## Emotional Job

- Intimacy: the ad feels like a message, not a brand broadcast.
- Curiosity: the incoming question opens a loop.
- Believability: casual phrasing can feel more real than polished copy.
- Momentum: the reply gives a simple action the viewer can copy.

## Copy Mechanism

The incoming message should ask about a practical situation. The outgoing reply should answer with a simple product habit. For this product, avoid "how did you lose weight" or other result claims.

## Safe-Zone Template Rules

- Keep the conversation bubbles between 25% and 63% height for Reels.
- The top story preview is decorative and can sit partly above the safe rectangle.
- Keep message text large enough for mobile and avoid long lines that wrap awkwardly.
- Do not put the only product mention in a bottom "seen" line.

## Watchouts

- Do not fake a real customer DM.
- Do not imply a visible body transformation or medical result.
