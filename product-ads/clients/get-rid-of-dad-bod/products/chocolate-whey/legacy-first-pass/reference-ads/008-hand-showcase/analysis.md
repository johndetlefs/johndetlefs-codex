## What The Ad Is Doing

This format makes the product feel like a desirable object. The hand gives scale and social context, while the gradient background makes the product feel expressive rather than clinical.

## Emotional Job

- Desire: the product looks like something worth holding up.
- Identity: the viewer imagines the product fitting their routine and taste.
- Lightness: soft gradients and mixed typography create a less serious supplement mood.
- Confidence: one clean product focus avoids category clutter.

## Copy Mechanism

The headline starts with the routine role, then the stylised line introduces the emotional bridge. The support line adds the concrete differentiator.

## Safe-Zone Template Rules

- Keep the headline and subhead inside 16% to 35% height.
- Keep the product label visible inside 38% to 64% height.
- Any hand crop can extend into the lower unsafe area as long as the product remains safe.
- Avoid thin white type over low-contrast gradients.
