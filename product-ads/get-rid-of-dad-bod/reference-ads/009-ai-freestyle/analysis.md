## What The Ad Is Doing

This format sells a product through sensory memory and mood. It barely explains the mechanics; it makes the product feel like a distinctive thing the viewer wants to experience.

## Emotional Job

- Craving: the headline evokes taste, smell, or seasonal memory.
- Novelty: the product feels more interesting than a commodity.
- Brand character: the design gives the product a point of view.
- Collectability: the diagonal ribbon and centered pack make it feel like a limited object.

## Copy Mechanism

The headline is the hook. The support copy should then ground the mood in one real product proof point so the ad does not become pure vibe.

## Safe-Zone Template Rules

- Keep the main sensory headline between 16% and 33% height.
- Keep the product label between 36% and 62% height.
- Bottom brand lines are decorative for Reels unless repeated higher.
- Diagonal ribbons should not cover safe-zone text.
